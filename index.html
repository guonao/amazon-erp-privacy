Amazon ERP Privacy Policy

This Privacy Policy describes how Amazon ERP collects, uses, stores, and protects data when interacting with Amazon Selling Partner API and related integrations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following business-related data:

  • Amazon store account identifiers
  • Order information
  • Inventory and listing data
  • Financial and settlement-related data
  • Advertising performance data, when authorized
  • Operational logs required for diagnostics, synchronization, and security

2. How We Use Information

We use the collected data only for legitimate internal business purposes, including:

  • Order and inventory management
  • Business reporting and analytics
  • Advertising performance analysis
  • Operational monitoring and troubleshooting
  • Improving workflow efficiency for our own seller operations

3. Data Sharing

We do not sell personal or business data. Data is not shared with unrelated third parties except when required to operate authorized integrations, comply with applicable law, or protect system security.

4. Data Storage and Security

We take reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect authorized data from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. Sensitive credentials are stored securely and access is limited to authorized internal use.

5. Data Retention

We retain business data only for as long as necessary to support operational analysis, reporting, compliance, and internal recordkeeping.

6. Your Rights

If you are an authorized business user and have questions regarding the data used in this system, you may contact us to request clarification, correction, or deletion where applicable.

7. Contact

For privacy-related questions regarding Amazon ERP, please contact the system operator through the business contact channel associated with this application.

8. Updates to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time to reflect operational, legal, or technical changes. The latest version will be published on this page.
